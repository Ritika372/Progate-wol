About

"Make stuff you love and talk about stuff you love and you’ll attract people who love that kind of stuff. It’s that simple.” — Austin Kleon
Hey NITJ fellas!
You’ve got some cool code project? Introduce it! You dance, paint, cook, make music, are into gardening…? Talk about it and show us what you made! If it’s more than one thing, that’s cool too! If you don’t have anything finished to show off, talk about the process — that’s at least as interesting as the finished result, anyway.
This should be a nice opportunity to see what kind of creative stuff our students are up to and maybe even lead to some collaborations outside of work. Worst case, we’ll just get to know each other a bit better.
